template<std::signed_integral size_type>
std::vector<size_type> pasta::internal::sharded::PairOccurrences< size_type >::occurrences

A list of block indices in which the content of the hashed block pair appears.

We're using an std::list here instead of an std::vector, since the reallocation upon insertion lead to issues during parallel access, when another thread tries to access the vector during reallocation.
